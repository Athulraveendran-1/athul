NMSM GOVT COLLEGE

  • NMSM (Neelikkandy Moidheen Sahib Memorial) Government College, located in Kalpetta, Kerala, is a major public higher education institution in the Wayanad district.


  • 🏛️ History & BackgroundEstablishment: Founded in 1981 by the Government of Kerala to provide affordable education to marginalized and rural communities.The Name: Named in memory of Neelikkandy Moidheen Sahib, a socially committed local figure whose family donated the land.
  • The Campus: Originally started in a rented facility, it moved to its permanent 25-acre (approx. 11 hectares) hillside campus at Vellaramkunnu in 1983. It sits right along the Kozhikode-Mysore National Highway (NH 212/766), about 4 km from Kalpetta town.Affiliation & Recognition: It is fully affiliated with the University of Calicut and is UGC-recognized and accredited by the NAAC.
  • 📚 Academic Programmes & DepartmentsThe college accommodates roughly 850–900 students with a faculty of over 45 professors.
  • It operates major departments across Humanities, Science, and Commerce:Undergraduate (UG) CoursesThe college has fully transitioned into the Four-Year Undergraduate Programme (FYUGP) structure framework under Calicut University. Popular streams include:B.A. Journalism and Mass CommunicationB.A. Development EconomicsB.A. History (The oldest degree department in the college)B.Com (Specializations generally include Cooperation / Finance & Insurance)B.Sc. Computer ScienceB.Sc. ChemistryPostgraduate (PG) CoursesM.ComM.A. Economics

  • 💵 Fee Structure & Seat Intake Because it is a direct government institution under the Government of Kerala, the academic expenses are highly subsidized:Tuition Fees: Government-merit seat fees range from roughly ₹3,150 to ₹4,000 total for standard B.A. or B.Com programs. Self-financing or specialized tracks can scale up to higher amounts depending on the lab requirements.
  • Annual Intake: The college admits roughly 200–250 fresh students across its degree programs every academic cycle.📝 Admission ProcessAdmissions strictly follow the reservation and merit rules governed by the state.Centralized Process: You must register online via the Calicut University Centralized Admission Process (CAP Portal).
  • Allotment: Seats are allotted over multiple rounds based on your 10+2 (Higher Secondary) marks and choice of preferences.
  • 🏫 Campus Facilities & Student Life Accommodation: A dedicated Girls' Hostel is available on campus. Boys' hostel facilities are not managed directly on-site, though private lodgings exist nearby.
  • Infrastructure: Features automated library services, a dedicated computer lab for Science/Language streams, a seminar hall, and multi-sport training grounds.Clubs: Active units of the National Service Scheme (NSS), National Cadet Corps (NCC), and various subject associations (like the History Association) drive student leadership programs.
